FLATUPGYM Security Video #1
威圧的な態度の侵入者
========================================

A cinematic warning sequence in the style of public service announcements. Monochrome, high-contrast lighting.

A silhouetted figure in dark clothing stands at a gym entrance. The figure's face is completely obscured by shadow - no facial features visible. Camera slowly zooms in. The figure's body language is tense and intimidating, with stiff, mechanical movements like a mannequin.

The figure attempts to step forward toward the entrance. Suddenly, a bold red "X" symbol appears overlaid on the screen with a sharp buzzer sound effect.

Text appears in heavy, bold typography (in Japanese):
"威圧的な態度や不適切な行動をとる方の入場はお断りしています。"

Screen fades to black, then smoothly transitions to bright, warm color footage of FLATUPGYM's welcoming interior with friendly members training.

Final text appears (in Japanese): "FLATUPGYM。世界一優しい格闘技ジム。"

Style: Public service announcement aesthetic, stark monochrome contrast, silhouette-only figures, mechanical movements, dramatic typography, warm color transition at end.

Duration: 20-25 seconds
